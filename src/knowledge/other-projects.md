# Other Projects

## Eventopia — Flutter Event Management App
A full-featured event management mobile app built in Flutter with Firebase backend.

**What it does:** Users can browse, filter, and book events. Free events book instantly, paid events go through Stripe. Automated booking confirmation emails via Firebase Functions + Nodemailer.

**Tech:** Flutter (Dart), Firebase Auth, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Firebase Functions, Stripe API, Provider + Bloc state management

**Key features:** Event browsing with filters, favorites, My Bookings section, Stripe payment integration, automated email confirmation, Help & Support with FAQs

**Timeline:** January 2025 — February 2025

## HealthBridge — Undergrad Final Year Project
A comprehensive healthcare web platform connecting patients and doctors — my undergraduate capstone project.

**What it does:** Appointment booking with video conferencing, ML-based disease prediction (heart, liver, diabetes, cancer), medicine alternatives finder (AlternaFind), mental health scoring module (MindOasis), electronic prescriptions

**Key modules:**

- MindOasis: Mental health scoring with counselor recommendations
- Appointment system with real-time calendar and automated notifications

**Timeline:** August 2022 — May 2023 (Undergrad capstone)

## Assignment Redesign Assistant — AI Tool for Faculty (CATLR)
A Claude-powered pedagogical tool built for Northeastern faculty to redesign assignments in the age of AI.

**What it does:** Faculty paste their assignment, Claude analyzes it using frameworks (Backward Design, TILT Transparency, UDL, Authentic Assessment, Scaffolding), identifies what aspects are appropriate or inappropriate for AI assistance, and automatically generates a redesigned version.

**Built with:** Claude API (Anthropic), prompt engineering, Claude Projects

**Impact:** Used by Northeastern faculty across Mills Institute and CSSH to redesign real assignments. Directly addresses the challenge faculty face: not wanting to ban AI but unsure how to integrate it responsibly.

**Timeline:** 2025 (ongoing as part of AI Instructional role at CATLR)
